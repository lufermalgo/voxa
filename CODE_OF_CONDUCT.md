# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Voxa is a technical project. The standard for participation is simple: be precise, respectful, and evidence-driven.

Constructive behavior includes:
- reporting facts clearly and with reproducible detail
- challenging assumptions with evidence, not opinion
- keeping debate technical and useful
- helping maintain product coherence and focus

Unacceptable behavior includes:
- personal attacks or harassment of any kind
- dismissing contributions without technical justification
- deliberately disruptive or off-topic behavior

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces: GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other community channel associated with Voxa.

## Enforcement

Maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions, comments, or other interactions that do not align with this standard.

Repeated or severe violations may result in a temporary or permanent ban from the project.

## Reporting

To report a violation, contact the maintainer directly via GitHub.
